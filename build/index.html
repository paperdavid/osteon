Health and Safety

Compliance with Strict Health, Safety, Security and Environmental Standards throughout all project stages.

To ensure safety at all times, TurkStream follows strict Health, Safety, Security and Environmental as well as Quality Control standards for the design, material, fabrication, installation, testing, commissioning, operation and maintenance of the Pipeline.
From the design phase right through to pipe production and construction, we are working with experienced engineers and international specialists to ensure the TurkStream Project is realised in accordance with pipeline industry standards. Our comprehensive risk analysis and hazard identification and our detailed inspection and maintenance systems will ensure that the gas pipeline meets international safety standards during its entire lifecycle. We will use safe and proven technology, as well as state-of-the-art materials and equipment, to guarantee safe operation for several decades.
